"What's up with the Jackalope?"

The Jackalope is my favorite cryptid creature. Cute but delightfully whimsical.

A weird little mythical critter that's grounded in reality (bunnies and antlers are totally real things), but put together in a way nature just never got around to.

To me, they represent the utilization of things that are mundane combined into something unique and strangely beautiful. And that speaks to me!
